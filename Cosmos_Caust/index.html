About The Team

We are Team Aurora, a group of passionate game developers dedicated to crafting immersive 2D experiences. Our journey began with a simple idea: to blend fast-paced platforming mechanics with a deep, emotional narrative about reclaiming one's humanity.

Gate of Aurora is our debut project, born from months of coding, pixel art design, and storytelling. We believe in games that challenge the player's skills while telling a story that resonates. Our vision is to create worlds where every pixel tells a story and every victory feels earned.
