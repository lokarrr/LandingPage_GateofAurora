The Origin of Vengeance

Backstory

Once an ordinary teenager, he was **abducted** and forced into a cruel experiment designed to push the boundaries of human evolution. Stripped of his freedom and humanity, he became nothing more than a lab rat for the scientists' twisted ambitions. After years of relentless modifications, the experiment reached its climax. His fragile body was **reforged with machinery**, his veins pumping with cold synthetic power.

Though the scientists celebrated their "success," he saw only betrayal. His humanity was stolen, his youth sacrificed, his future rewritten into steel and wires. Consumed by rage, he attempted to strike down the very man who orchestrated his torment, which is **his creator**. But he was overpowered, discarded like broken equipment, and cast into the depths of the underground ruins, a place where failed experiments rot and forgotten machines linger.

Down there, surrounded by shadows and echoes of other abandoned projects, his anger hardened into purpose. He vowed that one day, he would rise again, tear through the robotic legions guarding the laboratory, and **exact his revenge**. He would not rest until his creator felt the same pain of destruction that he had endured.

Mission

Fight your way back to the laboratory and **confront your creator**. Along the path, swarms of robotic enforcers, failed experiments, and automated defenses stand in your way, each one programmed to stop you from reaching the surface.

Every enemy you dismantle brings you closer to vengeance, but also reveals fragments of the truth: why you were chosen, what the true goal of the experiment was, and the terrifying scale of the scientist's plan. Your mission is not just to kill your creator but to **reclaim your stolen humanity**, or destroy what remains of it.

Game Mechanics & Rules

‚öîÔ∏è Game Mechanics

  • Player Role: Control the Gunner character with special skill activation.
  • Health System: Start with 5 Health Points (HP). Collect health items dropped by enemies.
  • Movement: Double jump ability (jump twice before landing).
  • Game Flow: Start at level beginning, enemies spawn randomly as you move toward exit.
  • Progression: Defeat ALL enemies in current map to spawn a key. Use key to unlock next level.
  • Level Structure: Multiple levels alternating between normal enemy levels and boss fights.
  • Enemy Types: Normal enemies chase and damage on touch (1 HP). Bosses shoot back with higher health.
  • Skill System: Activate special skill with "1" key (no cooldown).
